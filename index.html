About

How I think and how I work.

Who I am

I sit at the intersection of product thinking, brand strategy and operations. My background spans brand audits, ERP (Epicor) and hands-on testing / implementation work, which means I’m comfortable moving between commercial narratives and system reality.

I like to take ambiguous briefs, break them into structured workstreams, and move quickly from “we think” to “we know”. I’m used to working with incomplete data, tight timelines and multiple stakeholders, and I care a lot about clear documentation and clean handovers.
