About

How I think about problems and value.

How I think

I’m most comfortable when the brief is unclear. I like to take a messy situation, strip it down to first principles and rebuild it into a clear problem statement, options and trade-offs. I care about structured thinking more than flashy frameworks.

How I work

I move between analysis, conversation and documentation quickly: talk to people, pull the data that matters, sketch the flows, then turn it into a concise story that decision-makers can act on. I don’t hide behind jargon and I don’t produce slides that go nowhere.

Where I’m useful

Consulting-style roles where there’s real ambiguity: product strategy, PMO, pre-sales or internal projects that need someone to make sense of disconnected inputs, align stakeholders and push things over the line.
