About

A UX designer comfortable across the whole design lifecycle — from discovery and research through ideation, wireframing, validation and implementation.

How I think

I'm most comfortable when the brief is unclear. I take a messy situation, strip it down to first principles and rebuild it into clear problem statements, user flows and trade-offs. I care about structured thinking and real user understanding more than flashy frameworks.

How I work

I analyse user pain points through discovery sessions and research, then translate complex requirements into intuitive flows, wireframes and process maps. I collaborate closely with product managers, developers, architects and business stakeholders to deliver seamless experiences.

Where I'm useful

Designing digital experiences across fintech, SaaS and platform products — especially where there's real ambiguity, cross-functional complexity, or a regulated environment like banking that needs clarity and structure.

Case Studies

BNP Paribas — Banking Change & Governance

Banking · Requirements · Governance

My role
Project Management Consultant
Problem
Complex banking and technology change initiatives needed structured requirements and clear governance across many stakeholders.
Process
Facilitated requirement-clarification sessions, captured needs as BRDs and process flows, ran impact and dependency analysis, and tracked RAID with regular stakeholder updates.

Outcome Aligned business, security and delivery teams around complete, compliant documentation that kept change initiatives moving.

Epicor — Making the System Understandable

ERP · User Journeys · Workflow Design

My role
ERP Analyst / UX-minded researcher
Problem
People used Epicor daily, but few could see how their actions flowed through inventory, production and reporting — causing avoidable errors and low adoption.
Process
Ran discovery sessions with operational users, mapped end-to-end user journeys, and turned them into process diagrams and user-focused documentation.

Outcome More intuitive workflows and shared diagrams that helped non-finance users understand the impact of their choices and adopt the system with confidence.

BrandCollab — Platform UX for Creators & Brands

SaaS · Onboarding · Journey Design

My role
Product & Marketing Advisor / UX Designer
Problem
A creator-and-brand platform had experience gaps across onboarding, approval and reporting for three very different user types — creators, brands and administrators.
Process
Ran competitor analysis and user research to find the gaps, then designed onboarding journeys, user flow diagrams, process maps and low-fidelity wireframes, plus future-state journey maps.

Outcome Intuitive, future-state journeys that balanced business and user needs, with UX documentation and stakeholder-ready presentations that aligned founders on direction.
