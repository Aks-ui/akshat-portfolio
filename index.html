Case Studies

Short examples of how I structure problems and recommendations.

Sanofi – Lizipaina Brand Audit

Brand Strategy · FMCG

The situation: Legacy throat-care brand in a crowded shelf, with unclear differentiation and inconsistent in-store execution.

What I did: Structured a full-funnel audit across competition, consumer perceptions, retail presence and messaging, using a simple but rigorous framework.

Outcome: Clear strategic routes (defend, stretch, reposition) with concrete implications for pack, messaging and activation, rather than a generic “brand health” report.

Greggs – Brand & Customer Journey

Retail · Positioning

The situation: A loved high-street brand with strong habits but fragmented understanding of how customers actually experience it across touchpoints.

What I did: Mapped journeys across in-store, app and everyday behaviours, then linked those to perceptions of value, convenience and comfort.

Outcome: Identified where the brand wins by default and where it is effectively invisible, giving a clearer view of where to invest for long-term brand equity versus short-term promotions.

Epicor – Making the System Understandable

ERP · Systems Thinking

The situation: People were using Epicor every day, but very few could explain how actions in the system flowed through to WIP, inventory and margin.

What I did: Documented the end-to-end flow from sales order to invoice, highlighting where materials, labour and costs actually move.

Outcome: A simple set of flows and notes used by juniors and non-finance stakeholders to understand the impact of their choices in the system.
